02/Year-Round Space

Your Garden, Fully Utilised

Scotland's climate demands a garden room that is genuinely weatherproof and well insulated, not a glorified shed. Marshall & Co. builds garden rooms that are warm in January, comfortable in July, and solid enough to stay that way for twenty years.

Lee handles the full build from groundworks up: base preparation, framing, cladding, roofing, insulation, internal lining, electrics, and flooring. The room is designed to your specification, with the size, orientation, cladding material, glazing, and internal finish all agreed upon before a single foundation goes in.

Popular uses across Perthshire include home offices with a proper power supply and broadband, art studios, home gyms, and leisure retreats. Whatever the brief, Lee builds it to the same standard. Many clients also add hardwood or composite decking to connect the garden room to the house.

Garden Rooms FAQs

Common questions about our garden rooms service. Anything else on your mind, call us directly and we will be happy to help.

Do I need planning permission for a garden room?
Most garden rooms fall under permitted development rights and do not require planning permission, as long as they remain single-storey, do not exceed the height limits set out in Scottish planning guidance, and are not used as a primary dwelling. We will advise on your specific situation during the consultation.
Can a garden room be used all year round?
Yes. A well-built garden room with appropriate insulation, glazing, and heating can be used comfortably throughout the year, including during Scottish winters. We specify the materials and construction method to suit the intended use of the space.
What can a garden room be used for?
Garden rooms are used for a wide range of purposes including home offices, studios, gyms, hobby rooms, treatment rooms, and garden bars. The design and specification are tailored to how you intend to use the space.
How long does it take to build a garden room?
Build time depends on size and complexity, but most garden rooms are completed within two to four weeks once groundworks are done. A detailed programme is provided after the design and scope are agreed.
Can you connect electricity, plumbing, or heating?
Yes. We can coordinate the connection of power, data, lighting, and heating as part of the project. For plumbing, we work with trusted subcontractors where required. All installations are carried out to the relevant standards.
What materials do you use for the structure?
Construction methods vary depending on the design. We work with timber frame, SIP panel systems, and traditional block construction depending on what is most appropriate for the intended use, budget, and aesthetic. Options are discussed at the design stage.
Will a garden room add value to my property?
A professionally built, well-specified garden room generally adds usable square footage and is attractive to buyers, particularly where it functions as a home office or additional living space. The extent to which it adds monetary value depends on the local market and the quality of the build.
How do I start the process?
Get in touch via phone or the contact form and describe what you have in mind. We will arrange a site visit, take note of the space available and any access constraints, and come back to you with a proposal and full quotation.
