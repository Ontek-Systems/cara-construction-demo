03 / Why Choose Marshall & Co.

Blairgowrie is Regular Territory

Blairgowrie and Rattray sit on either bank of the River Ericht. The older parts of Blairgowrie have solid Victorian sandstone along Perth Street and Allan Street, and around the Wellmeadow, the central square at the heart of the town. These are properties with genuine character and they repay proper renovation work rather than quick fixes. Rattray on the east bank tends towards more modest interwar and post-war housing with its own set of structural considerations.

Lee covers Blairgowrie and Rattray throughout the year. He knows the range of properties here. Older stone builds require a different approach to more recent timber-framed development, and the eastern Perthshire climate means moisture and drainage issues appear regularly in both. He assesses each property independently before committing anything to paper.
