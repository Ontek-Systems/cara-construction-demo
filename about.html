02/Our Story

Craft Built Over Twenty Years

Marshall & Co. was founded on a straightforward belief: that the standard of a home improvement is only as good as the people carrying it out. Over two decades, that belief has shaped a team with deep roots in the Perthshire construction trade, working across everything from traditional period properties to contemporary new builds throughout Perth and Perthshire.

Every project is handled by experienced tradespeople who take genuine pride in what they produce. There are no shortcuts, no unfamiliar subcontractors turning up unannounced, and no handoff between teams who have never met. You get a consistent crew, a clear point of contact, and a finish that reflects the effort that went into it.

That consistency is why the majority of work at Marshall & Co. comes through repeat clients and personal referrals. When you trust a company with your home, you deserve to know exactly what you are getting.

02/Our Promise

Six Things That Never Change

Every project is different. These six things are not.

02.1/Our Promise

A Team You Can Trust

Your project is delivered by a skilled, trusted team working to one consistent standard, with a single point of contact throughout. You will never be left chasing someone you have never met about decisions on your home.

02.2/Our Promise

Structural Integrity First

Every extension and renovation is built to last. No shortcuts on structure, no compromise on what holds the building together. The finish is only as good as what sits beneath it.

02.3/Our Promise

Bespoke Joinery, Made for Your Space

Fitted kitchens, staircases, built-in wardrobes, media walls. Everything is crafted precisely to your space and specification, not pulled from a catalogue and forced to fit.

02.4/Our Promise

Honest Timelines

You receive a realistic start date and a realistic finish. If circumstances change, you hear about it directly from your point of contact, never weeks after the fact.

02.5/Our Promise

Matching Existing Character

Older Perthshire properties have character worth preserving. Lee has spent twenty years learning how to match sandstone detailing, period proportions, and original finishes so new work sits seamlessly alongside old.

02.6/Our Promise

A Clean, Respectful Site

Your home is treated as such throughout. Tools are packed away each evening, working areas are kept tidy, and disruption to your daily life is kept to the absolute minimum from start to finish.

03/Our Approach

Every Detail Is Considered

A lot of contractors can build an extension. Fewer think about how natural light falls in the afternoon, whether the proportions of the new room match the rest of the house, or how the transition from old to new will feel when it is done.

That level of consideration is what separates a good job from a great one. It is built into how Marshall & Co. works.
